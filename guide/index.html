Guide

Keys & Lock

You'll have two keys, one for the building and one for the apartment. The apartment door can be a little tricky to open: there's a metal cover with a slit which can turn. Be sure the slit is vertical so you can fit in the key.

Shower

When showering I recommend to set the water stream to the highest level, otherwise it's very tricky to control the temperature.
