Location and getting there

Location on Google Maps

From the main train station

Take the "S-Bahn" to Rennweg (it leaves every ~5 mins from platform 2)
Don't take any of the "westbahn" trains (the blue-green-white ones) as those are more expensive.
Or take a taxi for ~12€

From Erdberg bus terminal

Take the U3 underground to "Landstraße". Then take the "S-Bahn" to Rennweg: Take any train that leaves from platform 1 or 2 except for the "westbahn" trains (the blue-green-white ones). Rennweg is the next station.
Or take a taxi for ~8 €

From the airport

There are trains running from the airport to the city. The easiest option is the S-Bahn S7, which will take you directly to the Rennweg station. This train runs twice per hour, at :18 and at :48. Other trains run at :03 and :03 - with these trains you'll have to change trains at the main train Station (Wien Hauptbahnhof) A ticket will cost you 3.90 €.
Or take a taxi for ~25€

Finding the apartment

If you're at the Rennweg station, go up the escalators (not the stairs). You'll be near a bigger intersection. On the opposite side of the intersection there's a yellow fast food noodles stand. Close to it you'll see a "BIPA" store. Pass the BIPA store on the right and you'll be on the right street (Aspangstraße) - The apartment is 200 m down the street, at Aspangstraße 15, apartment 3.

Getting around

Vienna has a very good public transport system. You can buy day passes at the S-Bahn station or any underground station. Single tickets are also available on trams and buses.

To get to / from the apartment you'll maily use the S-Bahn train or tram 71.
The S-Bahn can take you to the central station, the main train station and the station "Prater".
Tram 71 will get you directly to the inner district and take you along the famous ring road where you can just hop off to see one of the many famous sights.
